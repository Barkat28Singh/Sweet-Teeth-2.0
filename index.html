About Us

We were inspired by Barkat's father and his love for anything sweet. We at Sweet Teeth make our candies from scratch. The candy making process is shown in the video on the right. Although manufacturing our own candies is our speciality, what makes us even more special is our wide range of international candies from all over the world.
